About Me

    Hello! My name is Caleb Early and I'm currently majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Math at Bridgewater College.

    When I was young, around middle school age, my cousin used to teach me 3D modeling, but I was never very good at it. In response to this I dabbled in different hobbies trying to find something that I could be better than him at, until I stumbled across coding.

    At the start I wasn't really that good, but I realized how fun it was and started to spend almost all my time trying to figure out how to make games.

    It's been 8.5 years now since I first started, and I haven't regretted the journey even for a second.

    Coding is my dream, passion, hobby, and life and if I'm ever at my computer there's a good chance that I'll be working on something.

    My main fascination is combining math with programming to see what I can create, but I love to push the boundaries of what JavaScript in the web browser can do.
