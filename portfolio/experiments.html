Procedural Tree Generator

Randomly generate a tree based on a set of rules.

The idea for this project came from looking up at some trees in my backyard and thinking, could I simulate the structure of trees?

After the COVID-19 Pandemic hit we had a lot of time at home, and I would often go outside to admire nature and think.
